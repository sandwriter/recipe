Description

Egg noodle is a simple and fun recipe to make. Fresh made egg noodle is delicious, and can be frozen for future use.

Total Time: 1 hour

Difficulty Level: 2 / 5

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of water

Steps

  1. Place eggs, flour, salt and water in Kitchenaid bowl
  2. Mix the ingredients in the lowest speed for 10 minutes. Make sure everything are mixed uniformly to eye
  3. Rest the dough covered with tower for 30 minutes
  4. Use pasta presser to form the thickness of the noodle to your liking, anywhere between 5-8 is good
  5. Run the dough through pasta cutter. Thin width cutter is my favorit
  6. Any noodle that will not be consumed should be dabbed with corn starch, and frozen immediately
  7. Throw the fresh noodle to boiling soup. Cook for 7-9 minutes. Stir ocassionally to avoid sticking
