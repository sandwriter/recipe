Description

Slow cooked Texas-style brisket is extremely tender and juicy. It is one of the most delicious recipe that I enjoy. The only caveat is brisket typically comes in 5+ pounds, and that is a lot of food.

Total Time: 8-10 hours

Difficulty Level: 2 / 5

Ingredients

  • Brisket: Costco sells brisket at ~$5/pound at 10+ pound package
  • Generous amount of salt
  • Salt-free Dash™ Orginal Seasoning Blend
  • Generous amount of black pepper
  • Advocado oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F
  2. Wash brisket and pat dry with paper towel
  3. Cut brisket into big pieces of similar thickness
  4. Trim the fat cap to about 1/4 inches
  5. Trim the ragged or discolored edges
  6. Rub oil over exposed muscle
  7. Splatter salt over all surfaces
  8. Splatter Salt-free Dash™ Orignal Seasoning Blend all over
  9. Splatter black pepper all over
  10. Place the seasoned brisket on a cooking rack or mesh on a roast pan
  11. Cook for 8-10 hours depending on thickness
  12. Turn over every 3-5 hours, and splash water or mist to keep exposed muscle moist
  13. Rest in room temperature for 30 minutes after done cooking
  14. Slice against the grain for optimal tenderness for serving
